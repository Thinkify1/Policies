🌐 Zoom Integration for Whop – User Guide

Welcome to Zoom Integration for Whop, your seamless solution for managing Zoom meetings with Whop integration. Whether you’re on the Free or Pro plan, this guide walks you through every feature - so you can get the most out of your membership, manage private and recurring meetings, and stay organized effortlessly.

🚀 Getting Started

1. Choose Your Plan

Upon signing in, you’ll be prompted to select a plan:

Free Plan

Instant access to basic features like viewing and managing your existing Zoom meetings.

Pro Plan

Unlock advanced features including creating meetings within the app, private member sharing, and recurring meetings.

➡️ After choosing a plan, Pro users are guided through a quick and secure payment process.

2. Connect Your Zoom Account

Next, you’ll be asked to authorize your Zoom account:

  • Follow the on-screen steps to securely connect Zoom.

Once connected, your meetings will automatically sync with Community Meetings.

All meeting actions are powered through Zoom—fully secure and seamless.

✅ 3. You’re All Set!

Once Zoom is connected, jump into your dashboard:

Free Users:

View and manage existing Zoom meetings.

Pro Users:

Create new meetings, schedule recurring sessions, and share with all members or only a few - all within the app.

📅 Main Features

1. Meeting Management

🟢Free Plan

  • Create Meetings: Use the Zoom app directly to create meetings (not available within Community Meetings).
  • View Meetings: All upcoming and past meetings auto-sync into the Whop dashboard.
  • Join Meetings: Click any meeting to join instantly from your dashboard.
  • Edit Meetings: Update meeting title, date, and time directly within the app.
  • Delete Meetings: Remove any synced meeting if no longer needed.

🔵Pro Plan (All Free features included, plus more)

  • Create Meetings: Easily create Zoom meetings from inside the app.
  • Share with All Members: One-click sharing - your meeting instantly appears in every member’s dashboard.
  • Set Meeting Details: Choose title, time, date, and other settings within a single streamlined interface.
  • Recurring Meetings: Schedule meetings to repeat daily, weekly, or at custom intervals.
  • Private Sharing: Share private meetings with a pastable link. Great for one-on-one or small group sessions.

2. Membership & Subscription Management

  • View Your Plan: Go to the Membership section in your Whop profile to see your current plan and billing details.
  • Upgrade to Pro: Use the Upgrade tab to compare features and upgrade securely in-app.
  • Manage Billing: Update payment methods, view invoices, and manage subscriptions easily.

🔐 Security & Privacy

  • Data Protection: All data is encrypted and handled securely using modern protocols.
  • Meeting Privacy: Only members you share with can see private Zoom links. Your privacy is our priority.
